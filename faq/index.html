How do I apply for staff?

You can apply for staff by doing an interview but there are some requirements that you need to meet before applying.

What are the server rules?

You can find the server rules in the #rules channel. Make sure to read and follow them to ensure a positive experience for everyone.

How can I report a problem?

If you encounter any issues or see someone breaking the rules, please report it to a staff member.

Can I promote my own server?

No, promoting other servers is not allowed. Please respect this rule to maintain the integrity of our community.

What events do you host?

We host a variety of events including gaming tournaments, art contests, and community hangouts. Check the #announcements channel for upcoming events.

Can I change my username?

Yes, you can change your username in your account settings. However, please avoid using inappropriate or offensive names.
